I am Dasun Sooriyabandara

As a graduate from Uva Wellassa University of Sri Lanka with a degree in Industrial Information Technology, I have cultivated a diverse range of interests and experiences that define who I am. My academic journey, leadership roles, and creative passions all contribute to my drive to excel in the industry.

My academic journey began at Maliyadeva College Kurunegala, where I developed a strong foundation in my studies. I furthered my education at Uva Wellassa University, focusing on Industrial Information Technology. My dedication to academics is reflected in my commitment to understanding the complexities of technology and its applications in the industrial sector.

Currently, I am working as an Associate Quality Assurance Engineer, where I am passionate about finding new things and exploring automation testing. This role has provided me with valuable insights into maintaining high standards in technology projects and has fueled my enthusiasm for innovation and efficiency. Additionally, my passion aligns with UI/UX design. I am drawn to this field because it allows me to merge my current work and technical knowledge with my creative talents. Using my block pen for drawing, I enjoy expressing my artistic visions and integrating them into user-centric designs. Writing also provides me with a platform to articulate my thoughts and ideas clearly.

Beyond academics and professional work, I have embraced leadership roles and extracurricular activities that have enriched my experiences. As the captain of the carrom team at Uva Wellassa University, I led my team to numerous victories and honed my skills in strategy and teamwork. This role taught me the importance of perseverance, collaboration, and leading by example.

Creativity is a significant aspect of my life. I find joy in drawing and writing, both of which serve as outlets for my artistic expression. These hobbies not only enhance my creative thinking but also complement my career aspirations in UI/UX design by allowing me to visualize and communicate innovative solutions effectively.

In my leisure time, I indulge in vintage items, films, songs, and literature. I have a particular affinity for mysteries and vehicles, which adds excitement and intrigue to my hobbies. These interests not only provide relaxation but also inspire me to think critically and explore new perspectives.

Overall, my journey as a graduate and my diverse range of interests have shaped me into an individual who is driven, creative, and motivated to excel in the field of Industrial Information Technology. My academic background, professional experience, leadership roles, and creative pursuits all contribute to my aspiration to reach the highest echelons of the industry and beyond.
